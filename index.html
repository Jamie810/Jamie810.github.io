About Me

My brief introduction

Welcome to my homepage! I'm Jamie Su, an aspiring researcher who loves sports and music as much as photonics. I'm currently pursuing my MPhil in Microelectronics at HKUST(GZ), where I work under the guidance of Prof. Yeyu Tong and Prof. Yuzhe Ma. My research interests center on photonic design automation and laser technologies, thriving at the intersection of hardware and algorithms. I believe that transformative innovation emerges from bridging physical systems with computational intelligence.
